## Decisions Made

1. **Model-centric entry point over procedural:** Model.query() feels more natural and active than Query() constructor
2. **Reserved name approach over descriptors:** Simple, clear, documented (vs. complex __getattr__ patterns)
3. **Expand reserved set to all builder methods:** Future-proof against all planned builder methods (query, metrics, dimensions, where, filter, order_by, limit, execute, to_sql, using)
4. **Field ownership validation at method level:** Raises TypeError immediately when mixing fields, preventing silent bugs
5. **Private _model attribute:** Uses underscore prefix to signal internal API, init=False to keep dataclass interface clean
