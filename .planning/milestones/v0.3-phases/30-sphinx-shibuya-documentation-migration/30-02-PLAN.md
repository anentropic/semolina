  <action>
Convert both tutorial pages from Markdown to RST following the conversion patterns in 30-RESEARCH.md. This is a FORMAT conversion -- do NOT change any content, code examples, or prose.

**Conversion rules (apply to both files):**

1. **Headings:** `# Title` becomes RST underline with `=`, `## Section` with `-`, `### Sub` with `~`

2. **Tab blocks** (`=== "Name"` with indented content) become:
   ```rst
   .. tab-set::
      :sync-group: warehouse

      .. tab-item:: Snowflake
         :sync: snowflake

         [content indented 6 spaces from left margin]

      .. tab-item:: Databricks
         :sync: databricks

         [content indented 6 spaces from left margin]
   ```
   - Snowflake/Databricks tabs: `:sync-group: warehouse`
   - pip/uv tabs: `:sync-group: installer`
   - Backend extra tabs (Snowflake/Databricks/Both): `:sync-group: backend`
   - Each `.. code-block::` inside a tab-item must be indented further (9 spaces from left)

3. **Admonitions:** `!!! tip "Title"` becomes `.. tip:: Title` followed by blank line and 3-space-indented body

4. **Inline code:** Single backtick `` `code` `` becomes double backtick ```` ``code`` ````

5. **Internal links:** `[text](path.md)` becomes `:doc:`text <path>`` (drop .md suffix)

6. **External links:** `[text](https://...)` becomes `` `text <https://...>`_ ``

7. **Material button:** `[text :octicons-arrow-right-24:](path.md){ .md-button }` becomes `:doc:`text <path>``

8. **Grid cards** (first-query.md "See also" section):
   ```rst
   .. grid:: 1 1 2 2
      :class-row: surface
      :gutter: 2

      .. grid-item-card:: Card title
         :link: path/to/page
         :link-type: doc

         Card description text.
   ```

9. **Code blocks:** ` ```python ` becomes `.. code-block:: python` followed by blank line and 3-space-indented code

10. **Bold text:** `**text**` stays as `**text**` (same in RST)

**installation.md specific:**
- Has 3 tab-set groups: installer (pip/uv), backend (Snowflake/Databricks/Both), and the admonition nested inside the pip tab
- The `!!! tip` admonition is INSIDE the pip tab-item -- indent it accordingly (6 spaces for tab content + 3 for admonition directive body)
- The Material button at the bottom becomes a simple `:doc:` link

**first-query.md specific:**
- Has 4 Snowflake/Databricks tab-sets (sync-group: warehouse)
- Has 1 grid card section in "See also" with 3 cards
- Has Material icons in heading (`:material-*:`) -- remove the icon syntax, keep the text

After creating each .rst file, delete the corresponding .md file.
